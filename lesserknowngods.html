Gods that you may have never heard of before

Atlas was a titan condemned to hold the celestial heavens by Zeus after the Titan War.

Heracles, The hero who was became a god and made immortal after he completed the 12 labours.

Amphitrite, she was married to Poseidon and became the queen of the seas.

Asclepius, he is god of medicine and all aspect medical arts.

Pan, is the God of nature, shepherds, flocks and of mountain wilds.

Nike, is the goddess of victory and a close companion of Zeus.
