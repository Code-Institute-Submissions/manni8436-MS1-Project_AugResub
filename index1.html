Below are three most famous Greek Gods
that are known all over the world.

Zeus, God of Sky and Thunder,
the most famous power was
he could throw lighting bolts
and he had a horse called
Pegasus to carry the thunder
bolts, he also trained a eagle
which could collect lighting
bolts, Zeus lived on Mount
Olympus and is the youngest
out of all the sibling to be
born.

Poseidon, God of the Seas,
the second oldest and also
brother of Zeus, Poseidon
live in Mount Olympus but
also lives in the oceans and
he has a trident with him in
which he can control the
oceans and also cause
earthquakes on land.

Hades, brother of both Zeus
and Poseidon also the oldest
out of the three but he doesnt
live in Mount Olympus with the
other Gods, Hades lives in the
Underworld because he drew
the shortest straw and became
the God of the Underworld.
