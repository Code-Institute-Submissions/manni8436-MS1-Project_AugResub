Below are three famous Greek Gods
that are known all over the world.

Zeus, King of the Gods, God
of the Sky and Thunder, Zeus
lived on Mount Olympus and
is the youngest sibling to be
born.

Poseidon, God of the Seas,
the second oldest and also
brother of Zeus, Poseidon
live in Mount Olympus but
also lives in the oceans.

Hades, God of the Underworld
the brother of both Zeus and
Poseidon also the oldest out
of the three but he doesnt live
in Mount Olympus with the
other Gods, Hades lives in the
Underworld.
