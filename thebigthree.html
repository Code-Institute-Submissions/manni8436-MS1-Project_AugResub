Below are three most famous Greek Gods that are known all over the world.

Zeus, God of Sky and Thunder, the most famous power was he could
throw lighting bolts and he had a horse called Pegasus to carry the
thunder bolts, he also trained a eagle which could collect lighting bolts,
Zeus lived on Mount Olympus and is the youngest out of all the sibling
to be born.

Poseidon, God of the Seas, the second oldest and also brother of Zeus,
live in Mount Olympus with all other eleven Olympians but also lives in
the oceans and he carries a trident with him in which he can control
the oceans and also cause earthquakes on land.

Hades, God of the Underworld, brother of both Zeus and Poseidon
also the oldest out of the three but he doesnt live in Mount Olympus
with the other Gods, Hades lives in the Underworld as he drew the
shortest straw between himself and his two brothers, he also has a
three headed dog called cerberus that helps him guard the gates
to the Underworld.
